About Us

Get to know the Smart Traffic App creators.

Hey there, we are the Smart Traffic Crew. We are three students from the University of Waterloo enrolled in ECE. We worked together to develop this app that allows the traffic flow in major intersections to run smoother and decrease the road rage we all have sometimes. This project was inspired by Patrik and Sarvath when they wanted to experiment with image processing. Sarvath thought of wanting to improve the traffic flow in his neighbourhood and learn how autonomous vehicles communicate with traffic lights. So after some discussion they both chose to work on this project. When it came to making it a full project, Sathira stepped in. The front-end god helped us in making this side project into an actual product.
