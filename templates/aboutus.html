About Us

Get to know the Smart Traffic App creators.

Hey there, we are the Smart Traffic Crew. We are three students from the University of Waterloo enrolled in ECE. We worked together to develop this app that allows the traffic flow in major intersections to run smoother and decrease the road rage we all have sometimes. This project was inspired by Patrik and Sarvath when they wanted to experiment with image processing. Sarvath thought of wanting to improve the traffic flow in his neighbourhood and learn how autonomous vehicles communicate with traffic lights. So after some discussion they both chose to work on this project. When it came to making it a full project, Sathira stepped in. The front-end god helped us in making this side project into an actual product.

My name is Sathira Katugaha, aka King Katugaha. I am a Computer Engineering student in UW. I aided in the development of the website and format of the functions in the MATLAB script. I spent my first two co-ops in SAP and the next one running a whole dev team by myself. Other than coding front end applications (which is my main speciality) I enjoy going on runs, ranting about Politics and Kanye, turn up with the bois on Saturdays and express my intense thoughts on Twitter.

My name is Patrik Beqo. I am a Computer Engineering student in UW. I aided in making the MATLAB algorithm to detect/track the vehicles and the design of the website. I spent my first two co-ops in the medical industry where I was an IT specialist and QA analyst. I have since upgraded to a Software Developer role at Ritual. In my free time I enjoy going on hikes, playing sports, and watching movies.

My name is Sarvath Sharma, aka Shawarma King. I am an Electrical Engineering student in UW. I aided in the computer vision, image processing and the integration of Python and MATLAB. I am also known as the kid who pushed the Python libraries to the GitHub repo to get more cred. My first two co-ops were at Solink as a QA Specialist and Junior Developer and then worked at WATONOMOUS where I was a Power Systems Member. In my spare time I enjoy working out, gaming, being a lil weeb and scrolling through Reddit for some fresh memes.
